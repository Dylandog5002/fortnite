What is Vbucks?

Fortnite, like other video games, has an in-game currency, known as V-bucks. When a player accomplishes a mission or challenge, Fortnite awards the player with V-bucks and other rewards in-game items. what is a battle pass?

The Battle Pass is a featured item in Fortnite Battle Royale! It offers players a chance to unlock in-game cosmetics, V-Bucks, and more! You will gain experience by playing the game which earns you levels (up to 100) that grant you rewards.
