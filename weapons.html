Twin Mag SMG

The Twin Mag SMG is a fast firing submachine gun which has 2 magazines attached to one another, meaning every other reload is faster.

Heisted Run 'N' Gun SMG

The Heisted Run 'N' Gun SMG is fast firing submachine gun. Holding the weapon will give the player a Slap Effect and will reload the weapon while sprinting. The weapon reloads one bullet a time, and reloads 2 bullets per second.

Heisted Blink Mag SMG

The Heisted Blink Mag SMG is a fast firing submachine gun which has 2 magazines attached to one another, speeding up every other reload. Reloading with this weapon will also grant the player a Zero Point Dash effect for a short duration.

Tactical Pistol

The Tactical Pistol is a fast firing fully automatic pistol with a tight spread. It functions very similar to an SMG.

Cobra DMR

The Cobra DMR is a semi automatic, fast firing rifle with a low power scope, that fires quick moving projectiles. It has a large magazine and can deal quick successive damage over range.

Dragon's Breath Sniper

The Dragon's Breath Sniper functions identically to the Bolt-Action Sniper Rifle, in that it is weapon which fires a single, fast moving, projectile bullet, which is affected by gravity. The weapon is fitted with a Scope for long range engagements but has only one bullet in its magazine, punishing inaccurate fire. Firing the weapon from the hip is very inaccurate also, having no indication on which direction the bullet will go. The special trait of the Dragon's Breath Sniper Rifle is that it can set wooden structures alight.

Heavy Sniper Rifle

The Heavy Sniper Rifle is a hard hitting, slow reloading Sniper Rifle. It deals a decent amount of damage to players, but even more to vehicles. It has poor hip-fire accuracy due to its class as a Sniper Rifle.

Kinetic Blade

Kinetic Blade is a sword melee weapon. Its basic attack is the Knockback Slash, which consists of two quick slashes, both dealing 35 damage. The second slash knocks opponents back a distance. Opponents hit by this attack are not given a shockwave effect, and will take fall damage if knocked back from a considerable height.

Its second attack, the Dash Attack, has you charge forward to deal 60 damage to opponents. This attack has 3 charges and a cooldown time of 10 seconds.
